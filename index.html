Jurgen's Bees

Pure, Raw, Local Honey

Jurgen's Bees is a local appiary, located in the heart of Kirkwood.

The honey is harvested multiple times a year, and harvests are kept seperate.
Because the harvests' are kept seperate,
you can see a wide range of color in the honey, along with different flavor profiles and textures.

Honey can be bought either by the honor system at the front of the house,
or at some local festivals!
